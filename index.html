Top 5 Cities to Visit

  • Barcelona:

    Known for its stunning architecture, particularly the works of Antoni Gaudí like the Sagrada Família and Park Güell.

  • Madrid:

    The vibrant capital city, home to the Royal Palace, Prado Museum, and bustling squares like Plaza Mayor.

  • Seville:

    Famous for its historic Alcázar palace, the impressive Seville Cathedral, and lively flamenco culture.

  • Valencia:

    Known for the futuristic City of Arts and Sciences, beautiful Turia Gardens, and delicious paella.

  • Granada:

    Renowned for the majestic Alhambra palace and the scenic views of the Sierra Nevada mountains.

Top 5 Things to do in Barcelona

  1. Explore La Sagrada Família in Barcelona:

    An iconic basilica designed by Gaudí, it's a masterpiece of architecture.

  2. Visit the Alhambra in Granada:

    A stunning palace and fortress complex with ornate Islamic architecture and beautiful gardens.

  3. Experience Flamenco in Seville:

    Witness a passionate flamenco show, an essential part of Andalusian culture.

  4. Wander through Park Güell:

    Another of Gaudí's creations in Barcelona, it's a colorful park filled with artistic structures.

  5. Tour the Prado Museum in Madrid:

    Home to a vast collection of European art, including works by Velázquez and Goya.
